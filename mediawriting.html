Natalia Mielczarek

Media Writing offers insight on news story writing skills, as well as press release/story formatting. In this class you will have to focus on story pitches as their deadlines come up quickly. There are also weekly news quizzes, which may sound like a lot to take on but it is easier than you think. Although the class is a bit difficult, it is quite an interesting and hands on class that will teach you how to write like an active journalist.
