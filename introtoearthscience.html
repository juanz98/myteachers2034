Intoduction to Earth Science online is a 14 module course. Every week you will have to complete a test, a quiz and an assigment for that lesson. Dr. Fedele is a little harder to work with,as deadlines are not excused for any reason. The course focuses on earth patterns and tectonic activity. You will also explore geological concepts and learn how to find the relative age of rocks.
