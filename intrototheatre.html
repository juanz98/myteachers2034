Introduction to Theatre fulfills a CLE requirement. This course is attendance heavy, as you discuss every assigned play in class. Failure to attend class could result in low exam grades, and missing extra credit assigments. Professor Rinehart is very passionate about theatre and it shows through her energetic and detailed lectures. With a total of 4 exams throughout the semester, this class should not be too difficult to manage.
